DOSSIER 02

Money Guide

The fastest way to get rich in GTA 6, organized by where you are in the story. Locked until we actually know the story — but here's what's reasonable to expect based on how Rockstar has done it before.

What we can say now

Based on patterns from GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 — not confirmed for GTA 6, just how Rockstar has approached single-player money before.

PATTERN

Story heists pay the most

In GTA V, the campaign heists (Jewelry Store, Union Depository, Paleto Bay) were by far the biggest single payouts, split between protagonists and crew. Expect GTA 6's story to work similarly with Jason and Lucia.

PATTERN

Side content adds up

GTA V and RDR2 both used optional side activities — armored trucks, random encounters, bounties — as smaller but steady income between story missions. Leonida will likely have its own versions.

PATTERN

Property is a slow burn

GTA V let Franklin buy businesses that paid out over time rather than immediately. If GTA 6 includes property investment, treat it as a long-term play, not a quick payout.
